Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Instant Invoice is free forever and released under the MIT license. There are no paid tiers, no trials, and no hidden costs.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up, login, or email required. Open the app and start creating invoices immediately.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays in your browser's localStorage on your own device. Nothing is sent to a server, because there is no server.

Can companies use it?

Absolutely. It includes VAT/Org/Tax IDs, OCR payment references with checksum validation, IBAN/BIC bank details, multi-currency and discounts — everything a business invoice needs.

Is it open source?

Yes — the full source is MIT licensed and available on GitHub. Contributions are welcome.
